What is This?

Compound Dividends

When others Buy and Sell their points, you will earn Dividends proportionate to how many points you hold. These Dividends are awarded in ETC, so you have a couple of options. If you choose to compound your Dividends, the ETC you have earned from others will be used to buy more Points, thus meaning you could earn more at a faster rate.


Withdraw Dividends

If you build up a nice amount of Dividends, you may choose not to buy more Points, but instead withdraw the ETC to your wallet. Should this be what you desire, tapping "Withdraw Dividends" is your answer.


Activate Divies

Divies is a Smart Contract connected to Commonwealth, which receives ETC and distributes it to holders of Points. One can contribute to Divies either by sending ETC directly to it's Contract Address, or by using a Dapp or service like the Broadcaster.


Activate RainMaker

RainMaker is a special Smart Contract: It provides a guaranteed Price Floor for the Commonwealth Vault - RainMaker can only buy and receive more Points - it can never sell, spend or send them anywhere at all. Learn More Here via the Medium Post about Rainmaker.
